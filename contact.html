How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Enquire

Call or visit the school office to enquire about seat availability and admission criteria for the desired class.

2

Get Form

Collect the admission form from the school office. Fill in all required details carefully.

3

Submit Documents

Submit the completed form along with required documents: birth certificate, previous marksheets, transfer certificate and photos.

4

Confirmation

After verification and interaction, receive your admission confirmation and fee payment details from the school office.
