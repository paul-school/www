Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2002

Paul International School was established in July 2002 by Mr. Ramanjit Singh Johar with a vision to provide quality education that blends academic rigour with character development. Located near Jal Bhawan on Sitapur Road, Lakhimpur Kheri, the school has grown from a small institution into one of the most trusted CBSE-affiliated schools in the region.

Over two decades, we have nurtured thousands of students who have gone on to excel in medicine, engineering, civil services, arts and entrepreneurship. Our alumni are a testament to the school's commitment to producing not just academically strong individuals, but responsible, compassionate citizens.

The school is managed by the Paul Educational Society and is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. We offer classes from Nursery to Class XII with Science and Commerce streams at the senior secondary level.

Our campus features modern classrooms, well-equipped science and computer laboratories, a library, sports grounds and dedicated spaces for arts and cultural activities — all within a safe, nurturing environment where every child is known by name.
