Why Choose Us

A School That Cares About Every Child

Founded in July 2002 by Mr. Ramanjit Singh Johar, Paul International School has grown into one of the most trusted CBSE-affiliated schools in the region.

📚

Strong Academic Foundation

CBSE curriculum from Class I to XII with experienced PGT, TGT and PRT faculty ensuring conceptual clarity.

🌱

Holistic Development

Sports, arts, music, dance and cultural activities alongside academics to develop well-rounded individuals.

🏛️

Modern Infrastructure

Smart classrooms, science labs, computer lab, library and sports facilities in a safe, nurturing campus.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family-Spirited Culture

A warm, inclusive environment where every student is known by name and supported to reach their potential.

What We Offer

A Complete Learning Experience

From foundational primary years to competitive board preparation — we offer a structured, enriching journey for every student.

🔬

Science & Technology

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry and Biology labs. Computer science with hands-on coding and digital literacy programmes.

📐

Mathematics & Commerce

Strong maths faculty from primary to Class XII, with dedicated coaching for board and competitive exam preparation.

📖

Languages & Humanities

English, Hindi and Social Science taught with a focus on communication, critical thinking and cultural awareness.

🎨

Arts, Music & Dance

Dedicated teachers for fine arts, classical music and dance — nurturing creativity and self-expression in every child.

🏅

Sports & Physical Education

Annual sports meets, inter-house competitions and daily physical education to build fitness, teamwork and discipline.

🎭

Co-Curricular Activities

Debates, science exhibitions, cultural nights, Republic Day parades and Founders Day celebrations throughout the year.
